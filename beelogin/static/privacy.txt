Information We Collect

We collect very limited information, only what is necessary for you to use our service:

Third-Party Authentication (OAuth): If you log in using a third-party service (e.g., Google, Facebook, GitHub), we receive the email address and basic authentication information required from that provider to verify your identity.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for authentication purposes. This includes:

Creating and managing your user account.

Allowing you to log in to our website.

Verifying your identity when you access your account.

We do not use your email for marketing purposes.

Cookies

We use cookies on our website. These cookies are strictly necessary and are used only for authentication purposes (such as keeping you logged in). We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any outside parties.
